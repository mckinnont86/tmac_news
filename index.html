📅 Seasonal Maintenance Calendar
❄️ Winter · Dec – Feb
Cold Weather Priorities
Monthly: Check/replace furnace filter · Test smoke & CO detectors · Check for ice dams

December: Insulate exposed pipes · Check weatherstripping on doors/windows · Stock emergency supplies (flashlights, batteries, generator fuel) · Service snowblower

January: Check attic insulation for heat loss · Inspect crawl space for moisture/freezing · Keep cabinet doors open under sinks in freezing temps

February: Schedule HVAC spring tune-up · Check roof for ice damage · Inspect basement walls for cracks from freeze/thaw cycles
🌱 Spring · Mar – May
Post-Winter Inspection
Monthly: Test smoke & CO detectors · Check HVAC filter · Inspect for pest activity

March: Inspect roof for winter damage (missing shingles, flashing) · Clean gutters of winter debris · Check exterior caulking and siding · Test sump pump before rain season

April: Service AC unit — clean condenser coils, check refrigerant · Flush water heater of sediment · Check deck/patio for winter heaving · Inspect driveway for cracks

May: Clean dryer vent duct · Check and clean window screens · Turn on irrigation, inspect for leaks · Reseed bare lawn patches · Trim trees away from house
☀️ Summer · Jun – Aug
Peak Season Upkeep
Monthly: Check AC filter (monthly in heavy use) · Inspect for pest entry points · Check irrigation system

June: Deep clean refrigerator coils · Inspect and clean bathroom exhaust fans · Check exterior paint for peeling · Seal deck/wood surfaces

July: Check attic for proper ventilation and heat buildup · Inspect window & door seals · Clean range hood filters · Lubricate garage door hardware

August: Flush and inspect water heater anode rod · Check sump pump · Schedule chimney inspection before fall · Check caulking around tubs/showers
🍂 Fall · Sep – Nov
Pre-Winter Prep
Monthly: Replace furnace filter · Test smoke & CO detectors · Check weatherstripping

September: Clean and store outdoor furniture · Drain and winterize irrigation system · Inspect roof before winter · Check attic insulation levels · Service heating system

October: Clean gutters after leaf fall · Drain outdoor hose bibs and shut off valves · Caulk exterior gaps and cracks · Reverse ceiling fans clockwise for heat retention · Check fireplace damper and chimney cap

November: Insulate pipes in unheated spaces · Check sump pump before ground freezes · Test generator · Stock emergency kit · Arrange snow removal service if needed
🔁 Always-On Monthly Tasks
HVAC
Replace furnace/AC filter (1–3 months) · Check vents for blockage
Safety
Test smoke detectors · Test CO detectors · Check fire extinguisher
Water
Check under sinks for leaks · Inspect toilet for running · Check water heater for rust/drips
Exterior
Walk the perimeter — look for new cracks, pest activity, standing water
Appliances
Clean refrigerator door seals · Check dishwasher spray arms · Run garbage disposal with ice
Electrical
Test GFCI outlets (kitchen/bath) · Check for tripped breakers · Inspect extension cord use
